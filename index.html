Pluto-Bitcoin DCA is an automated, tokenized Dollar-cost Averiging smart contract on Waves Blockchain. It works slightly different than the Genesis DCA dapp. The main difference is that it's NOT based on the rounds. You can deposit and withdraw without term limits and the accumulated funds from DCA are available to manually claim.

PLUTO token is used as a Base Capital. Dapp will DCA only from compounded interest to Bitcoin. All accumulated funds are available to manually claim. There are no term lock and no rounds.

Read more on our Telegram and Medium.
